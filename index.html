Dr. Xun Zhou was an Associate Professor in the Department of Business Analytics, Tippie College of Business at The University of Iowa. He was also an affiliated faculty member with the Interdisciplinary Graduate Program in Informatics (IGPI) and the Applied Mathematical and Computational Sciences (AMCS) programs. Xun received a Ph.D. in Computer Science from the University of Minnesota, Twin Cities in 2014, advised by Prof. Shashi Shekhar. Prior to that, Xun received his M.S. and B.E. degrees both in Computer Science and Technology from Harbin Institute of Technology, Harbin, China in 2009 and 2007, respectively. He is a senior member of the IEEE.
